Padel Coaching Programme

VPadel is proud to have tied up with the leading Padel Academy in the world, The `Hello Padel Academy. We believe that to enjoy the sport & play it for a long time, technical training is important. Our endeavour in this tie-up is to provide a systematic & professional method of teaching. We will provide online as well as offline coaching for Beginners to Advanced Level. We will also provide coaching programmes to coaches who would like to get into the profession of Padel Coaching.

Beginners – for Juniors & those who are just starting Padel. Mainly group training.
Intermediate – for Players with basic skill level who want to go to the next level. Combination of group & individual training.
Advanced – Players with advanced skill levels, who would like to work towards playing on the National, International Circuit. Individual training with the focus onspecific skill & strategy development is provided.

For more details please contact :- training@vpadel.in (need to create the id)

Coaching Venues :- VPadel Centre, The Acres Club. Mumbai


We specialise in the following areas:

- Increasing court Utilisation
- Coach Education
- Increasing membership numbers
- Online and offline sports marketing assistance
- Hosting & organising events, tournaments
- Coaching sessions & community engagements
- Attracting sponsorships
- Engaging Local schools & Junior academies
